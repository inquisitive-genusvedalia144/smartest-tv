## 🔐 Network setup

For the app to work, your computer and TV usually need to be on the same Wi-Fi network.

Check these points if the TV does not respond:

- The TV is turned on
- The TV is connected to Wi-Fi or Ethernet
- Your PC is on the same network
- The TV allows remote control from local devices
- The TV has the right app permissions enabled

If your TV uses a first-time pairing prompt, accept it on the TV screen.

## 🛠️ Basic troubleshooting

If the TV does not respond, try these steps:

1. Confirm the TV is on.
2. Check that your PC and TV use the same network.
3. Restart the app.
4. Restart the TV.
5. Check your TV’s remote control or network settings.
6. Try again with a simple command like volume up.

If the app cannot find your TV, make sure the IP address is correct and that the TV is ready for remote access.
